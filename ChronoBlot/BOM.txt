Bill of Materials
Design: ChronoBlot
Author: Andrew Schamp

IC1	DS3231 or DS3232 RTC (http://www.maxim-ic.com/datasheet/index.mvp/id/4627 or http://www.maxim-ic.com/datasheet/index.mvp/id/4984/t/al)

J1, J2	1x4 0.1" square pin headers
